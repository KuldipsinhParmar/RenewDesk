Everything in one place

Track every asset your team manages — domains, hosting, maintenance, backups, tasks, and clients — from a single dashboard.

Domains

Monitor expiry dates, registrars, and renewal costs. Get alerted 30, 15, and 7 days before any domain expires.

Hosting

Track server plans, providers, renewal schedules, and monthly costs across all your hosting accounts.

Maintenance (AMC)

Manage annual maintenance contracts with start/end dates, yearly pricing, and client payment status.

Backups

Schedule and track backup cycles — daily, weekly, or monthly. Mark done to advance the cycle automatically.

Clients

Manage your client portfolio with company info, country, and a direct link to all their projects and assets.

Tasks & Hours

Log work tasks, track hours, and filter by project or date. Keep a full activity record per client project.

Automated email alerts before anything expires

RenewDesk's built-in email engine sends digest alerts 30, 15, and 7 days before any asset expires. Your team is always ahead — never scrambling at the last minute.

  • Daily cron job scans all assets automatically
  • Alert threshold configurable per team (7, 15, 30 days)
  • CC multiple team members on any notification
  • Test email from Settings in one click
